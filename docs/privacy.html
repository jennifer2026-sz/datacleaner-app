Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 2026

Zero Data Collection

DataCleaner CLI is a local-first tool. We collect absolutely nothing.

  • ✅ All scanning and redaction happens on your machine
  • ✅ Documents never leave your computer
  • ✅ No telemetry, no analytics, no usage tracking
  • ✅ No cloud servers involved in any processing step
  • ✅ No account required for the free tier

What DataCleaner Does NOT Do

  • ❌ Does NOT upload your documents anywhere
  • ❌ Does NOT send your data to any cloud API
  • ❌ Does NOT store your PII findings
  • ❌ Does NOT collect your IP address or location
  • ❌ Does NOT use cookies (it is a CLI tool)

License Activation

When you activate a Pro or Team license, the license key is validated locally against a cryptographic signature. No network call is made for validation.

Audit Logs

Audit logs are stored locally in ~/.datacleaner/audit/. They contain metadata about findings (category, type, confidence) but never the actual PII content. You control these files entirely.

GDPR Compliance

Because DataCleaner processes data entirely on your local machine, it operates as a data processing tool under your control, not as a data controller or processor. We never touch your data — you remain the sole data controller.
