Run dc license activate YOUR-KEY. Validation is completely offline — no phone-home, no internet required. We use Ed25519 digital signatures to verify your license locally in under 1 millisecond.

Frequently Asked Questions

Is this really a one-time purchase?
Yes. Your Pro or Team license never expires. You own the version you purchased forever. The optional annual renewal ($49/$99) gives you continued access to new features and PII pattern updates.
What happens if I don't renew?
Nothing. Your license continues to work with the version you have. You just won't receive new updates released after your update period ends. If you renew later, you get all updates released since your last renewal.
Does the license work offline?
Yes. License validation uses local Ed25519 signature verification. No internet connection is ever required — not for activation, not for usage, not for anything.
Can I transfer my license?
Pro licenses are per-user and non-transferable. Team licenses cover up to 5 named users within the same organization.
What about enterprise / volume licensing?
For teams larger than 5, or organizations needing custom PII patterns, on-premise deployment support, or SLAs, email contact@getdatacleaner.com.
Why not a monthly subscription?
DataCleaner runs on your hardware using your GPU. We don't have server costs to recoup. Charging a monthly fee for something that doesn't cost us anything ongoing doesn't sit right with us — or our users.
