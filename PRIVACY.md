# Privacy Policy — DataCleaner CLI

**Last updated: May 2026**

## Zero Data Collection

DataCleaner CLI is a **local-first tool**. We collect absolutely nothing.

- ✅ All scanning and redaction happens on **your machine**
- ✅ Documents never leave your computer
- ✅ No telemetry, no analytics, no usage tracking
- ✅ No cloud servers involved in any processing step
- ✅ No account required for the free tier

## What DataCleaner Does NOT Do

- ❌ Does NOT upload your documents anywhere
- ❌ Does NOT send your data to any cloud API
- ❌ Does NOT store your PII findings
- ❌ Does NOT collect your IP address or location
- ❌ Does NOT use cookies (it's a CLI tool)

## License Activation

When you activate a Pro or Team license, the license key is validated locally
against a cryptographic signature. No network call is made for validation.

## Audit Logs

Audit logs are stored **locally** in `~/.datacleaner/audit/`. They contain
metadata about findings (category, type, confidence) but **never** the actual
PII content that was detected. You control these files entirely.

## GDPR Compliance

Because DataCleaner processes data entirely on your local machine, it operates
as a **data processing tool** under your control, not as a data controller or
processor. We never touch your data — you remain the sole data controller.

## Changes to This Policy

If we ever change this policy (e.g., to add optional cloud features), we will:
1. Update this document
2. Make any cloud features strictly **opt-in**
3. Never change the default behavior from local-only
